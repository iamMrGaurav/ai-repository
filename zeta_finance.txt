Professional Overview:
Professor Kerry Walsh is a distinguished academic with practical, hands-on expertise combined with strong theoretical foundations. He specializes in research and development with real-world applications that benefit both society and industry. His career successfully bridges commercial and academic domains, with particular expertise in:
- Near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS)
- Thermal time analysis
- Machine vision technologies
- Agricultural automation, especially mango harvesting systems
